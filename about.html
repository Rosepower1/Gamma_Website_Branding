About Gamma

The joy of sharing life!

Gamma strives to make sure it is both easy and fun to share your favorite moments. We respect your privacy and allow you a platform to interact with the family members and friends of your choice. There is no interaction with social media unless you choose to do so and you are in complete control of your albums.


How to add members

Got to your profile page and select "Invite New Members." Once you do this, you will be given the option of inviting your friends on Facebook, Instagram and Twitter. You may also click on "Send email" to those who are not active on social media, but would like to join your private group. You will receive notifications when an invitee accepts and is now active.

How to post and share your photos

To take a photo in Gamma, tap the camera icon on your homescreen. Once you take the photo, you will see options to add a filter or a caption. Click save when the photo is how you would like it and the "Ready to Share?" button will appear. If you click this button, you will see options to share to certain groups or to social media sites.
